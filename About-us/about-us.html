About Us

Powering the next generation of innovators.

Hallucinations Digital

Founded by Ammar Hairi, THINK4R is the brainchild of Hallucinations Digital, a freelance venture dedicated to web development. Hallucinations Digital, operating as a one-person powerhouse, epitomizes the passion and expertise behind THINK4R.

FOCUS

At THINK4R, we are driven by a singular focus: empowering organizations and individuals to excel in the Fourth Industrial Revolution (4IR). Catering to the specific needs of forward-thinking companies, we serve as a dedicated online training center, emphasizing 4IR education. For knowledge enthusiasts eager to explore the realms of 4IR, THINK4R stands as a comprehensive Learning Management System (LMS), providing an immersive learning experience.

PRESENTLY

Currently, THINK4R stands as the result of the relentless dedication of one individual, Ammar Hairi. As we move forward, our vision extends beyond solo efforts. In 2024, we aspire to expand our team, welcoming skilled professionals onboard to collaborate in the continued growth and maintenance of THINK4R, ensuring its stability and innovation.

COLLABORATION

A significant milestone in our journey occurred in June 2024, with the impactful launch of THINK4R in collaboration with esteemed Malaysian universities such as UKM, UUM, and UtEM. These collaborations have allowed us to offer specialized 4IR courses, elevating the educational experience within THINK4R.

CONTRIBUTE

THINK4R takes immense pride in being the pioneering force in online 4IR education within Malaysia. Our aspiration is to foster a tech-savvy Malaysian community, deeply engaged with the rapidly advancing global technology landscape. With THINK4R, we not only bridge educational gaps but also contribute to Malaysia’s presence in the swiftly evolving world of technology.

JOIN US

Join us on this transformative journey, where education meets innovation, and together, let’s shape a future where every individual and organization thrives in the digital age.
