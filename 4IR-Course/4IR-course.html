Cloud Computing

A technology that enables users to access and store data, and run applications over the internet instead of on local hardware. It offers on-demand computing resources, enhancing flexibility and scalability. Services like storage and servers can be accessed remotely, boosting efficiency, reducing costs, and enabling seamless collaboration in today's digital landscape.
